The Rise of Obesity Related Cancers in New York State

By: Jenna Bagcal

The New York State Department of Health compiled data which shows the upward trend of obesity from 2000 to 2010.

According to the data, the rate of obsesity has risen from 17.7 percent to 24.5 percent, which is a 40 percent increase.

The data also shows the most common obesity-related cancers that adults are diagnosed with.
